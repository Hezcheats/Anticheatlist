Free VPNs


Proton VPN

Proton VPN is a popular free VPN service.

SoftEther

SoftEther is a different kind of VPN. It emulates an ethernet connection with the help of the software. It is known to bypass a lot of antivpns on servers.

Client Suggestions


Forge Ghost

Raven B+ is the best forge ghost client for Minecraft till date. It is stable and reliable and is pretty popular among Hypixel players.

Hypixel client

Raven bS is the best free hypixel client (tho I wouldn't use any free hypixel clients!)

Forge Blatant

Liquidbounce client is the best forge blatant client. It is the most trusted out of all the clients on MimHax and is well supported by community made scripts.

Standalone Blatant

Vestige V3 is the best free blatant client ever. It is a great multiserver client and is now open-source.
